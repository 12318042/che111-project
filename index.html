What is solar Energy                            


Solar energy is the radiant light and heat from the Sun that is harnessed using a variety of
technologies, including photovoltaic cells, solar heating, and solar thermal energy. This
renewable energy source is abundant and sustainable, making it an increasingly important
part of the global energy mix.



Pro's and con's of solar Energy                            


Solar energy's pros include sustainability, reduced emissions, and low operating costs. It
fosters energy independence and is versatile in deployment. However, challenges include
intermittency, high initial costs, land use concerns, and resource-intensive production.
Addressing these issues is crucial for maximizing solar energy's role in a sustainable energy future.



Use and How to use solar Energy-Solar Panel and cells                            


Solar panels, composed of photovoltaic cells, harness sunlight to generate electricity. These
cells, typically made of semiconductor materials, produce an electric current when exposed
to sunlight. The photons in sunlight energize electrons, creating a flow of electricity. The
generated power can be used directly or stored for later use, offering a clean and renewable energy source.
